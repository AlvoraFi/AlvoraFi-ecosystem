# ALVORAFI

> Own Your Financial Life in Total Privacy.

AlvoraFi is the first privacy-native neo bank built on Solana, combining zero-knowledge finance, Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) AI agents, and private darkpool trading into a unified financial platform.

Designed for users who value privacy, security, and complete ownership of their assets, AlvoraFi enables confidential payments, private investing, intelligent portfolio management, and shielded DeFi—all without exposing financial activity on public ledgers.

---

## 🚀 What is AlvoraFi?

Traditional finance protects privacy through closed banking systems.

Traditional DeFi exposes every wallet, balance, transfer, and trading strategy on public blockchains.

AlvoraFi introduces a new financial paradigm:

* 🔒 ZK-Shielded Assets
* 🤖 TEE-Protected AI Agents
* 🌑 Darkpool Swaps
* 💳 Privacy Banking
* ⚡ Solana Performance

The result is a banking experience where users retain ownership while minimizing unnecessary exposure of financial data. Modern privacy systems on Solana increasingly combine zero-knowledge proofs, confidential execution, and private transaction infrastructure to protect user activity.
